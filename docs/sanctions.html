Sanctions & Restricted Regions

Last updated: 20 February 2026

1. Compliance Overview

SolSow complies with international sanctions programs administered by:

  • OFAC — United States Office of Foreign Assets Control
  • EU — European Union restrictive measures
  • UK — United Kingdom sanctions (OFSI)
  • Australia — Australian Sanctions Office (DFAT)

2. Geographic Screening

The App uses multiple signals to detect the user's geographic region:

  • SIM card country code (MCC)
  • Network operator country
  • Device locale settings
  • Timezone configuration
  • Geocoder reverse lookup (when location permission is granted)

If any signal indicates a sanctioned or restricted region, access to the App is blocked with an explanatory message.

5. VPN and Circumvention

Users must not use VPNs, proxy services, or other circumvention tools to access the App from sanctioned or restricted regions. Doing so may violate applicable sanctions laws.

6. Blockchain Compliance

The App's optional wallet integration connects to the Solana blockchain. Users are responsible for ensuring their use of blockchain technology complies with all applicable laws and regulations in their jurisdiction.

7. Changes

This sanctions policy may be updated to reflect changes in international sanctions programs. We monitor sanctions developments and update our restricted regions list accordingly.
